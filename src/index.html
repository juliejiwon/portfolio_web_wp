loeyB

2021.01 - 2021.06

KAIST 'Application Start-up Program' Participant

Role : Project Leader, Designer

We live our life moment by moment, but most of the moments disappear from your memory. loeyB helps you collect experiences that are important to you easily by using your digital traces, so you can connect the dots of your life and understnd youself better leading to a more satisfying life.

website

When journaling, people have difficulty remembering what they did and feel alot of pressure writing down events or emotions. LoeyB takes the pressure off remembering experiences and the stressful recording process and makes the journey easy and even fun!

The onboarding pages and tutorial introduces the main concept of the loeyB app, collecting and connecting experiences making your digial universe.

Stardust are made by swiping through your past photos and labeling the moment with emotions and any tags you want by typing them in yourself.

The main screen of the application shows your stardusts(experiences) organized by category and keywords that you tagged. Organization of your experiences will help you with confidence and self-awareness
